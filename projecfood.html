Sustainable Cooking

What is Sustainable Cooking?

Sustainable cooking refers to practices that prioritize environmental health, social responsibility, and economic viability in food preparation and consumption

What are Ways to be Sustainable?

The main ways are using local ingredients, plant-based meals, and minimizing food waste to benefit the environment.
